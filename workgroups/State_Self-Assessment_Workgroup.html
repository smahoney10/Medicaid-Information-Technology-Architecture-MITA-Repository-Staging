MITA 4.0 Initiative

MITA State Self-Assessment (SS-A) Workgroup

The goal of MITA 4.0 is for states, territories, and CMS continuously assess, align, and collaborate on Medicaid Enterprise strategy and outcomes to better drive and demonstrate program outcomes.

The State Self-Assessment (SS-A) Workgroup is spearheading the development of a re-imagined SS-A process that is outcome based, less burdensome, and more useful to states and CMS. The SS-A Workgroup will work towards achieving the MITA 4.0 goal by developing a new SS-A tool and co-developing a new Maturity Model with MITA NextGen.

The membership of the SS-A Workgroup comprises of subject-matter experts from states, federal agencies, industry, vendors, and technology integrators. The SS-A Workgroup continues to recruit relevant subject-matter experts so please contact us at MITAGovBoard@cms.hhs.gov, if you are interested in joining the workgroup.
