MITA 4.0 Initiative

MITA NextGen Workgroup

The MITA NextGen Workgroup is dedicated to advancing the Medicaid Information Technology Architecture (MITA) framework. This team, composed of Medicaid business and technical experts from State Medicaid Agencies (SMAs), vendors, and the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS), is focused on evaluating and enhancing MITA 3.0 components for inclusion in MITA 4.0. Having completed the initial phase of drafting an architecture vision, the team is now refining the Business Architecture, with plans to address Information Systems and Technical Architectures in upcoming phases.

Vision

  • Refine, refocus, and repurpose MITA

Goals

  • Enhance Relevance and Accessibility: Make MITA more meaningful and accessible for stakeholders.
  • Reduce State Burden: Streamline processes to ease state agency operations.
  • Enable Automation: Incorporate automation to improve efficiency.
  • Integrate with APD and Certification: Align MITA with Advanced Planning Document processes and outcomes.
  • Align with Trends: Release guidance reflecting current healthcare and IT trends

Recent Activities Update

  • Reviewing MITA 3.0 Components:
    • Conducting an in-depth analysis of current business architecture components to identify enhancements that align with evolving Medicaid system needs.
    • Identifying Enhancements: Proposing improvements to enhance MITA's user-friendliness, flexibility, and
    • Drafting a New Capability Model and Accompanying Capability Matrix: Creating a comprehensive capability model and matrix that align with SMA needs and industry best practices, offering a clear link to SMA and CMS prioity outcomes.
    • Improving integration of the Concept of Operations with standard SMA planning practices and roadmap development.
    • Updating the Business Process Model to include mappings of Member Management.
    • Aligning with Updates: Ensuring the capability model aligns with MITA maturity model updates and outcomes-based planning for seamless integration and implementation.

The NextGen Workgroup is making substantial strides in advancing the MITA framework, ensuring it continues to be an indispensable resource that empowers Medicaid Enterprise System users, planners, and developers to effectively serve enrollees and providers.

The NextGen Workgroup continues to recruit relevant subject-matter experts so please contact us at MITAGovBoard@cms.hhs.gov, if you are interested in joining the workgroup.
