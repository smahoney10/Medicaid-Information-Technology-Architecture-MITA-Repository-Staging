MITA 4.0 Initiative

MITA Communications Workgroup

The Communications Workgroup’s role is to increase interest and awareness of MITA 4.0 and provide ongoing updates to ensure the Medicaid Community has insight into the journey of its development. The workgroup publishes a quarterly newsletter and maintains the MITA Repository to share information to keep stakeholders informed of the MITA Governance Board’s activities and progress developing the new MITA 4.0. The workgroup also provides information and helps to market MITA at key workshops including Medicaid Enterprise Systems Conference (MESC). In addition, they explore options for publishing and sharing new MITA 4.0 materials.

Vision

  • Promote awareness of MITA at conferences, distribute news about MITA changes, and design an improved approach to publishing MITA guidance.

The membership of the Communications Workgroup comprises of subject-matter experts from states, federal agencies, industry, vendors, and technology integrators.
